Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my drone to the app?

To pair your drone with the app, ensure both devices are on. Then open the app, navigate to settings, and choose your drone from the device list.

My drone isn’t responding. What should I do?

If your drone isn’t responding, try restarting it. Turn it off, wait a few seconds, then power it back on. If the problem persists, check for updates or reset the drone.

How long does the battery last?

On average, our drones provide up to 30 minutes of flight time. To get the most out of your battery, keep your drone calibrated and avoid flying in extreme weather.
